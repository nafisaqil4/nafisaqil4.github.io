Research Experience

Undergraduate Thesis: A Micro-grid Framework for Grid Resiliency

Advisor: Assoc. Prof. Engr. Md. Rashidul Islam

Investigated micro-grid frameworks to enhance Bangladesh's grid resilience against faults. Modeled and simulated PV systems, bidirectional battery storage (BESS), and advanced inverter control using dq-transformation in MATLAB/Simulink. The proposed framework significantly improved the resilience index from 0.291 (conventional grid) to 0.657 (micro-grid) under simulated three-phase fault conditions.
