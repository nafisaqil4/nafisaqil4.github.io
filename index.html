About Me

My name is Anupam Chowdhury, and I have completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from International Islamic University Chittagong (IIUC) with a CGPA of 3.63. During my undergraduate studies, I explored advanced concepts in electronics, simulation, and computational modeling, which motivated me to apply engineering knowledge to real-world problem-solving. My undergrad thesis was on “A Micro-grid based Operational Framework for Grid Resiliency Enhancement in Bangladesh”.

I have hands-on experience with 3D modeling and simulation tools such as COMSOL, and I am particularly interested in Smart Grid, Optics, Quantum Computing, Materials, and emerging energy technologies. My passion for research and innovation drives me to continuously learn new skills and expand my expertise.

In the future, I aim to pursue a career in research and development, contributing to technological advancements and making a meaningful impact through engineering and scientific exploration.
